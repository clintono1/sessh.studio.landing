Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
1. Who We Are

Sessh ("we," "our," or "us") operates the studio management platform available at sessh.studio. We provide booking automation, client management, and communication tools to recording studios and their clients.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you use our platform:

  • Studio Owners: Name, email, phone number, business name, payment information, and booking configuration data.
  • Artists & Clients: Name, email, phone number, session preferences, and payment history.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, device type, browser, and IP address.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Sessh platform
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send SMS and email reminders related to your sessions
  • Deliver session files and invoices
  • Send platform updates and service notifications
  • Improve our product based on usage patterns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and booking a session, you consent to receive automated SMS messages related to your booking including confirmations, reminders, and file delivery notifications. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Twilio: SMS delivery. Subject to Twilio's privacy policy.
  • GoHighLevel: CRM and automation infrastructure.
6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, you may request a full data export within 30 days of cancellation. After that period, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To make a request, email us at info@sessh.studio.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. All payment information is processed by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@sessh.studio
