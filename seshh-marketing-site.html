Do I need to be technical to set this up? +
Not at all. Onboarding takes under 30 minutes. We walk you through connecting your domain, setting up your rooms and rates, and linking your Stripe account. If you get stuck, we'll jump on a call and do it with you.
What happens to my bookings if I cancel? +
Your data is always yours. If you cancel, you get a full export of your contacts, bookings, and files before your account closes. We don't hold anything hostage.
Can my artists book without creating an account? +
Yes â€” they can fill out a booking inquiry form without an account. An account gets created automatically when they pay their first deposit, and they receive a login email instantly.
How does the AI call answering work? +
When someone calls your studio number, an AI answers, answers common questions (availability, rates, location), and can send a booking link via text. It never misses a call, even at 3AM. You get a transcript of every call.
Does Seshh work for studios with multiple rooms? +
Yes. Starter supports up to 2 rooms, Pro supports up to 5, and Elite is unlimited. Each room has its own availability calendar, rate, and booking settings.
What's the transaction fee on bookings? +
Zero. We charge a flat monthly fee and that's it. Stripe's standard processing fee (2.9% + 30Â¢) applies to every transaction, but that goes to Stripe, not us. We never take a cut of your bookings.
Can I use my own domain? +
Yes. Every plan includes a free subdomain (yourstudio.seshh.studio). Pro and Elite plans support connecting your own custom domain so clients see your brand, not ours.
